Parque São Jorge

Parque São Jorge, also known as "Fazendinha," is Corinthians' historic headquarters and training center. Located in the Tatuapé neighborhood of São Paulo, this complex has been the club's home since 1928.

The facility spans over 158,000 square meters and houses multiple sports facilities, administrative offices, and social areas for club members.

Club Facilities

Swimming Pools

Olympic-sized swimming pool and recreational pools for members.

Sports Courts

Multiple courts for basketball, volleyball, and futsal.

Social Areas

Restaurants, event spaces, and recreational areas for families.

Memorial do Corinthians

The Corinthians Museum, located within Parque São Jorge, houses the club's rich history. Visitors can explore:

  • Trophy Room with all major titles
  • Interactive exhibitions about the club's history
  • Historic jerseys and memorabilia
  • Multimedia presentations of great moments
  • Special section dedicated to the Fiel supporters
